About ARIES

ARIES (Automation Robotics & Industrial Equipment Specialists) is dedicated to helping businesses in the industrial and automation sectors build high‑performing teams. We specialise in recruiting engineers, technicians and leaders with the expertise needed to design, build and maintain cutting‑edge equipment. From start‑ups to global enterprises, our mission is to connect top talent with opportunities that drive innovation.

Jobs We Hire

Automation Engineers

Experts in designing and implementing automated systems, including robotics programming, PLCs and control architectures.

Robotics Technicians

Hands‑on professionals who install, maintain and troubleshoot robotic arms, conveyors and industrial machinery.

Mechanical Designers

Engineers who create precision mechanical systems for manufacturing lines, custom tooling and electromechanical assemblies.

Control Systems Engineers

Specialists who develop PLC and HMI programs to integrate sensors, actuators and machine vision with production equipment.
